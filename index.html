Questions

FAQ.

Direct answers. If something isn't covered here, reach out and you'll get a real reply.

How is this different from SEO?

SEO targets Google's blue-link results. AI search ranking targets ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews — the engines buyers now use to make decisions. We optimize the entity graph, citation network, and answer-first content that large language models extract from, not keyword density or backlink volume.

Why pay-on-performance? What's the catch?

There isn't one. We charge $500 upfront to cover the audit, page build, and citation foundation. The $1,500 monthly only begins once you're cited #1 on at least three buyer-intent queries across major AI engines. If we don't deliver in 90 days, you get the setup back.

How long until I'm ranked?

Most clients are cited within 30 to 60 days, depending on competition density and existing digital footprint. AI engines refresh their training data and citation patterns on rolling cycles. We confirm rankings weekly across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews and share progress reports with you.

What if I don't rank in 90 days?

You get a full refund of the $500 setup, no questions asked. The monthly fee never starts until we've delivered, so the only money at risk is the setup fee. We're betting on ourselves because we know the system works when applied properly to a real local operation.

Do I own the page you build?

Yes. The page lives on your domain, the schema is yours, the citation assets are yours. If you cancel, everything stays. We don't gate access or hold your authority hostage. The one thing we keep is the methodology — the playbook is ours, but every output is yours.

What does "one client per niche per city" actually mean?

If we work with a dental practice in Port St. Lucie, we won't take on another dentist in that city. Same for HVAC, med spas, attorneys, and so on. This protects your investment — you're never competing against another client for the same AI citation slot. Slots are first-come.

Can you guarantee a specific search query?

We guarantee citations on a basket of buyer-intent queries we identify together during the audit, not a single phrase. AI engines vary their responses based on user context, conversation history, and time of day. Locking to one exact string would be dishonest. We target query patterns instead.

Why is the monthly only $1,500?

Because once the foundation is built, maintenance is mostly citation refreshment, schema updates, and ranking confirmation. We've productized the heavy lifting. Charging $5,000 a month would price out the local businesses this is built for. We'd rather work with twenty owners who pay on time than two who churn.

What happens if I cancel?

You stop paying, and we stop maintaining. The page, schema, and citations stay live on your domain — you own all of it. AI rankings hold for a few months on inertia, then erode as competitors invest. Most clients stay because the monthly cost is less than one new customer per month.

Do you work with businesses outside of Florida?

We're based in Port St. Lucie and prioritize Florida service businesses, but we'll take on out-of-state clients case by case. The system works in any English-speaking market. The practical constraint is that we visit local clients to understand the operation; remote clients get a longer onboarding call instead.

How is the audit free? What's in it?

We run 15 buyer-intent queries against ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews using your business name and category. You get a written report showing where you appear, where competitors appear instead, and the specific citation gaps causing it. Takes us about two hours. Costs you nothing.

What if my competitor signs up first?

Then we work with them, not you. There's no waitlist for occupied slots — once a niche-and-city pair is taken, it's locked for the duration of the contract. If you're considering this, the audit is the fastest way to claim your spot. Slots in dense markets move within weeks.
